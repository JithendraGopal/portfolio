About Me

Hi, I’m J Jithendra Gopal, an Electronics & Communication Engineer with a passion for transforming ideas into innovative digital experiences. My journey revolves around blending technology and creativity, whether it's through web design, mobile app development, or embedded systems. I specialize in UI/UX design, web development, and user experience design (UED), using tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and Fusion 360 to bring projects to life.

In my previous role as the Chief Executive Officer of IEDC at ASIET, I led initiatives that fostered innovation and entrepreneurship, empowering students and startups. As a freelance web developer, I have successfully delivered custom web applications and engaging user experiences for various clients, always aiming to merge functionality with user-friendly designs.

Beyond my professional work, I am actively involved in volunteering. I’ve been a part of the IEEE Student Branch and the NSS at ASIET, contributing to various initiatives that impact both the tech community and society. Currently, I volunteer for the Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Centre under Kerala Startup Mission, supporting entrepreneurship development.

With a strong foundation in embedded systems, PCB design, and Python programming, I am always eager to learn, grow, and contribute to technology-driven solutions that make a difference.

My Projects

Oxygen Concentrator

In response to the urgent need for medical oxygen during the COVID-19 pandemic, our team developed an innovative, cost-effective oxygen concentrator. This project aimed to address the rising demand for oxygen therapy among individuals experiencing severe respiratory symptoms associated with the virus.

The successful execution of this project was made possible through the collaboration of a dedicated team from Adi Shankara Institute of Engineering and Technology, Kalady, including:
