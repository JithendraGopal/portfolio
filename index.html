About Me

I’m J Jithendra Gopal, an Electronics & Communication Engineer passionate about Embedded Systems, VLSI Design, and hardware-software integration. I specialize in developing real-time embedded solutions, digital circuit designs, and FPGA-based projects using tools like ModelSim, Vivado, and KEIL. With experience in sensor interfacing, system validation, and PCB design, I enjoy building intelligent systems that bridge hardware with software. Alongside my core electronics work, I bring creativity through UI/UX design and web development, crafting intuitive digital interfaces using Figma, Adobe XD, and front-end technologies. As former CEO of IEDC at ASIET,I have conducted many technical events. As a freelance developer, I’ve delivered custom web applications that balance functionality with user-focused design. I’m also actively involved in the IEEE and NSS, and currently volunteer for Kerala Startup Mission, promoting entrepreneurship in tech communities.

My Projects

Oxygen Concentrator

In response to the urgent need for medical oxygen during the COVID-19 pandemic, our team developed an innovative, cost-effective oxygen concentrator.
